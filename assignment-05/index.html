Rice Krispies!

Ingredients

  • 5 Tbsp butter
  • 10 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal

Directions

  1. Spray a 9x13in baking pan and spatual with cooking spray
  2. Heat a large pot over low-heat and slowly add 8 cups of the mini marshmallows, stir continuously with spatual until completley melted
  3. Remove large pot from burner and add Rice Krispies Cereal to pot of melted mini marshmallows until almost combined
  4. Add remaining 2 cups of mini marshmallows into the pot and mix until incorporated
  5. Transfer mixture to baking sheet and press down
  6. Let cool completely
  7. Cut into squares or whatever shapes you prefer and enjoy!
